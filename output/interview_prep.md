## Strategic Advice
Tone & Focus  
- Be confident yet collaborative: emphasize team successes and your role as a technical leader.  
- Tie every technical story back to customer impact (e.g., “This feature improved diagnosis speed by X%”).  
- Showcase your continuous-improvement mindset: how you drove efficiency or quality through DBS/Lean.  

Red Flags to Avoid  
- Criticizing past processes without proposing solutions—focus on constructive improvements.  
- Over-emphasizing code over process in medical-device context: balance technical depth with compliance awareness.  
- Underestimating cross-discipline communication: highlight examples of working with hardware, QA, regulatory teams.  

Final Tip  
Prepare a concise 2-3 minute “firmware elevator pitch” that links your background, key achievements and passion for Leica’s mission. Close by expressing genuine enthusiasm for contributing to life-saving microscopy innovations—and you’ll leave a memorable, strategic impression. Good luck!
